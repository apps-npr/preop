1. ข้อมูลการผ่าตัด (Surgery Info)

Procedural Bleeding Risk Classification

อ้างอิง: CHEST Guideline 2022

Minimal Risk
  • Cataract surgery
  • Minor dental procedures (Extraction 1-2 teeth, Cleaning, Endodontic)
  • Minor dermatologic excision (Basal/Squamous cell carcinoma)
  • Bone marrow aspiration / Lumbar puncture
Low/Moderate Risk
  • Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy
  • Inguinal Hernia Repair
  • Appendectomy
  • Arthroscopy
  • Gastroscopy / Colonoscopy with biopsy/polypectomy
  • Coronary Angiography
  • Breast surgery (Lumpectomy/Mastectomy)
High Risk
  • Major Abdominal/Pelvic surgery (Cancer resection)
  • Thoracic surgery (Lobectomy, Pneumonectomy)
  • Major Orthopedic surgery (Hip/Knee replacement)
  • Vascular surgery (AAA repair, Vascular bypass)
  • Urological surgery (TURP, Nephrectomy, Bladder resection)
  • Liver or Kidney biopsy
Neuroaxial / Critical Site

*กลุ่มนี้เลือดออกเพียงเล็กน้อยอาจส่งผลรุนแรงถึงชีวิตหรือความพิการ

  • Intracranial surgery
  • Spinal surgery / Epidural anesthesia
  • Posterior eye surgery (Vitrectomy, Retinal surgery)
